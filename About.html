All About Bill Gates

    Overview about Gates

  • Date of Birth: October 28, 1955
  • Place of Birth: Seattle, Washington, USA
  • Nationality: American
  • Occupation: Business magnate, software developer, philanthropist, author
  • Net Worth: Approximately $114 billion (as of 2024)

    • Education

  • Born into a well-educated family with a lawyer father and a teacher mother.
  • Attended Harvard University, but dropped out in 1975 to pursue a career in technology.
  • Gates wrote his first computer program at age 13.

    • Key Achievements & Recognition

  • Named Time's Person of the Year (along with Melinda Gates and U2's Bono) in 2005 for their philanthropic work.
  • Ranked consistently on Forbes’ Billionaires List.
  • Author of books like "The Road Ahead" (1995) and "How to Avoid a Climate Disaster" (2021).
  • Awarded the Presidential Medal of Freedom in 2016 by President Barack Obama.
  • Significant work in climate change, funding clean energy innovations.

    • Personal life

  • Married: Melinda French Gates in 1994; divorced in 2021 after 27 years of marriage.
  • Children: Three children – Jennifer, Rory, and Phoebe Gates.
  • Enjoys reading, playing bridge, and a passion for technology and innovation.
  • Wealth Pledge: Bill and Melinda Gates, along with Warren Buffett, signed the Giving Pledge, committing to donate the majority of their wealth to charity.
